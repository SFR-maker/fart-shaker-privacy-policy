# Privacy Policy for Fart Shaker

**Last updated: April 20, 2026**

## Overview

Fart Shaker ("the App") is a simple entertainment app that plays fart sounds when you tap a button or shake your phone. This privacy policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

## Data Collection

**Fart Shaker does not collect any personal data.**

The App does not:
- Collect your name, email, or any personal information
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, camera, or microphone
- Use any analytics or crash reporting services
- Use any advertising SDKs or display ads
- Connect to the internet
- Store any data on external servers

## Device Permissions

The App only uses:
- **Vibration** — to vibrate your phone when a fart sound plays
- **Accelerometer (motion sensor)** — to detect when you shake your phone

Neither permission allows the App to access personal data.

## Third Parties

Fart Shaker does not share any data with third parties because no data is collected.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
